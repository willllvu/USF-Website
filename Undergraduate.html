Undergraduate Information

Students pursuing an undergraduate degree at USF have many opportunities and advantages open to them. The college's central location allows for internships, jobs, and education experiences that align with students' specific goals. Those who go into business have to complete an additional 36 credit hour business core that aids them in learning and understanding management responsibilities.

Requirements


While USF has its own general admission requirements, the Muma College of Business, being an "upper-level, limited access college", has requirements in addition to those. Students who apply to go into the college, therefore, must meet both sets of prerequisites to gain acceptance. Click to view the general requirements and the Muma College of Business honor pledge.

Majors


The USF Muma College of Business, depending on the program, requires around 18-30 credit hours of specialized study for major course work. The college offers 9 undergraduate majors:


  • Accounting
  • Advertising
  • Business Analytics and Information Systems
  • Finance
  • Global Business
  • Management
  • Marketing
  • Personal Financing Planning
  • Pre-med Dual Degree with Biomedical Sciences

To learn more about each major, check out USF's official page.

Minors


The USF Muma College of Business offers undergraduate students to choose from 6 minors:

  • Accounting
  • Business Analytics and Information Systems
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Finance
  • Management
  • Marketing

To learn more about each minor, check out USF's official page.

Study Abroad


USF offers numerous opportunities for students to discover business on the international level, with programs that often impact students view of the world around them and teaches them how to approach problems in a global marketplace.
